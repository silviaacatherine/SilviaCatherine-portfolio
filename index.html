01 — About

Where data
meets diplomacy

Data Science Major Int'l Relations Minor

Holaa! I'm Silvia Catherine — someone who has always been curious about how the world works, especially people, the systems around them, technology, ideas, and change.

That curiosity slowly grew into interests in Data Science, International Relations, design, and emerging technology.

By day, I'm training regression models. By night, I'm reading geopolitical briefings and calling it "self-care." My actual superpower is translating the chaos of world events into numbers, then making those numbers interesting to people who swore they hated statistics.

I believe data without context is just noise, and international relations without evidence is just vibes. Someone has to do both rigorously — might as well be me.

Alongside that, I love creativity in all its forms — music, storytelling, branding, building ideas, and creating things that feel both thoughtful and real. I'm still learning, still building, and still figuring things out — but this space holds pieces of that journey.
